Growth Tips

2025 best practices to rank higher

⭐
Reply to every review within 24h
Google rewards active engagement. Responding to all reviews signals trust and lifts your local ranking.
✏️
Post at least once per week
Regular posts keep your profile fresh. Mix updates, offers, and behind-the-scenes content for best results.
📸
Add new photos every week
Profiles with photos get 42% more direction requests. Upload real photos of your team, space, and work.
💬
Seed your own Q&A section
You can ask and answer your own questions. Use the Q&A tab to generate and post them yourself.
📍
Use local keywords naturally
Include your city and neighborhood in your description. "Orlando florist near UCF" beats generic descriptions.
🕐
Keep hours 100% accurate
Inaccurate hours hurt trust and ranking. Update holiday hours proactively every single time.
🏷️
Add all relevant secondary categories
Most businesses only set one category. Adding 3–5 relevant secondary categories expands your search reach significantly.
🔗
Build local citations consistently
Ensure your NAP (name, address, phone) is identical across Yelp, Facebook, Apple Maps, and industry directories.
